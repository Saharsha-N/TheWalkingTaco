So...What is it Made Of?

The taco car is made exclusively out of wood and non-toxic paint, ensuring a safe experience for your child and preventing you from getting a headache. It is composed of only 3 different parts:
____- Taco Body/Shell
____- Wheels
____- Sombrero

This simplistic design ensures that even if the toy breaks, there is no choking hazard created by the broken toy. In the unlikely event that a part of the toy breaks, such as the wheels or sombrero, our insightful usage of a dowel rod allows fixing to be a piece of cake. If something breaks, just:
____1. Ensure none of the pieces have splintered or been damaged
____2. Line part of dowel rod that contacts the broken piece with wood-grade super glue
____3. Place and hold two pieces together, allowing time for glue to cure
____4. Voila! Your toy is now fixed!
